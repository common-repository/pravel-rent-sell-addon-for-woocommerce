Pravel Rent & Sell Addon for WooCommerce is a flexible, open-source eCommerce solution built on WordPress. Sell & Rent anything, anywhere and make your way.

== Description ==

Pravel Rent and Sell for Woocommerce allow you to create a rentable product.

[Click here](https://www.codegrape.com/item/pravel-rent-sell-addon-for-woocommerce-pro/37064) to purchase the paid plugin

Working with Rentable products will be very easy. Making a Product available for both Rent and Sell and also managing their Stock never been easier before. Managing Sperate Price is also easy.

Activate the free Pravel Rent and Sell for Woocommerce plugin on a new or existing WordPress site, and set up a new store in minutes with:

- Product, Cart, and Checkout pages
- Secure payments by credit card and alternatives
- Easy Interface.
- Manage [separate price](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepTwo) for a product which will be on rent as well as available for sale.
- Choose to make products only [rentable or Both](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepOne) (Rent and Sell).
- [Notify Me](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepSeven) option for a customer to get a notification when a product is back in stock.
- [Pickup](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepEight) your order from store option
- [Add Days Block](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepFive) with price to rent Product for specific days block on discount.
- Feature to Sell [Used Products](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepFour) (Second Hand Products).
- Enable [special price for weekends](https://pravelsolutions.co.in/Pravel-Rent-and-Sell/setup/#SetupStepFive) and special days
- Different attributes for Products can be set for both Buy and Rent


= What You Can Do with Pravel Rent and Sell Add-on? =

It depends on you. We have created a feature that will allow making your product rentable. So there are no limitations on which type of product you can create. Either it can be Clothes, Books and so on... 

Plugin Has a Functionality that will notify customers when the product they wanted to buy/rent is back in stock.
